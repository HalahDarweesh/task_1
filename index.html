Chewy Chocolate Chip Cookies

Delicious, soft, and chewy chocolate chip cookies that are perfect for any occasion.
Easy to make and loved by everyone!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup (225g) unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup (200g) granulated sugar
  • 1 cup (220g) packed brown sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3 cups (360g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 cups (350g) chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. In a large bowl, cream together the butter, granulated sugar, and brown sugar until smooth.
  3. Beat in the eggs one at a time, then stir in the vanilla.
  4. Combine the flour, baking soda, and salt; gradually blend into the creamed mixture.
  5. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  6. Drop by rounded spoonfuls onto ungreased cookie sheets.
  7. Bake for 10–12 minutes, or until edges are lightly browned.
  8. Cool on baking sheets for a few minutes before transferring to wire racks.
